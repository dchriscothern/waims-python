The Platform · Live on Streamlit Cloud
WAIMS Python
Athlete monitoring and readiness — from daily signal to staff decision.

Performance staff at lower-resource programs spend their mornings aggregating wellness surveys, force plate exports, and GPS data into spreadsheets before they can make a single decision. WAIMS compresses that into a five-minute morning brief with clear flags, role-appropriate views, and a forward-looking forecast layer.

PythonStreamlit SQLitePlotly Random ForestOura API
What makes it different
Personal z-score baselines alongside absolute thresholds — not either/or
CMJ/RSI as primary fatigue signal, grounded in basketball-specific research
Role-based views: coach, sport scientist, GM, athlete
Oura API connector built as POC — wearable-ready architecture
Female athlete and basketball-specific research citations throughout
Say in an interview
"A readiness monitoring platform built for programs that can't afford Catapult. Morning data to staff decision in under five minutes."
The Commercial Wedge · Sandbox Demo Ready
WAIMS-GM
Front-office decision support — from roster question to structured recommendation.

GMs and coaches at lower-resource programs make roster, recruiting, and acquisition decisions by combining notes, film, and gut feel with no repeatable framework. WAIMS-GM gives that workflow structure — a dossier-based process that produces a documented, exportable recommendation every time.

How They Relate

Platform. Wedge. Extension coming.

Two distinct roles in one coherent system — with a third layer in development.

01
WAIMS Python is the foundation
The platform layer. Daily monitoring, readiness signals, role-based views, wearable ingestion architecture. Everything else builds on top of this data model and staff workflow.
02
WAIMS-GM is the commercial story
Takes the platform's player data and extends it into front-office decisions. Different buyer, different workflow — the clearest path to a real product conversation with a program.
A third layer — Basketball Match Analyzer — is in active development. It will add video intelligence and longitudinal biomechanical signals to the platform. It will be added to this portfolio when it reaches demo-ready state.
